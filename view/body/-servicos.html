Why Choose Us?

Comprehensive Care in a Single Location

Our dedication to early detection using the latest technology and resources is just part of what sets Lutheran apart. Our breast care specialists are here for you from your screening through the earliest diagnosis to treatment and recovery. We want to be your partners in health.

If you are diagnosed with cancer, we don’t refer you elsewhere. Instead, we immediately create a personal, collaborative,

Integrated Care Teams

Our cancer teams believe in open communication and collaboration in order to provide you the best breast treatment and care possible. Every breast cancer case is reviewed at a weekly conference by experts from radiology, surgery, pathology, pharmacology and oncology, as well as ancillary services such as physical therapy and psychological assistance, to ensure you are receiving the best care possible. This provides you with a unified care team that is focused on you and your recovery. Our entire team is dedicated to restoring your health.

Award-Winning & Accredited Care

When you choose Lutheran, you can be confident you’re receiving some of the highest quality care in the nation. Lutheran was the first Colorado hospital to earn accreditation from the National Accreditation Program for Breast Centers (NAPBC) for our Breast Care Center. Lutheran joins only 203 other hospitals nationwide that have earned this accreditation. The Center is also accredited by the American College of Radiology for mammography, breast ultrasound, stereotactic biopsy and ultrasound-guided biopsy and is a member of the National Consortium of Breast Centers
